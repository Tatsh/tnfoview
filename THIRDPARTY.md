# Third-party assets

## unscii-16.ttf

- **Location:** `src/fonts/unscii-16.ttf`
- **Project:** unscii by Viznut
- **Source:** <https://github.com/viznut/unscii>
- **Licence:** Public Domain (CC0)

unscii is a character-cell font with full coverage of the CP437 box-drawing and
block-element glyphs, whose shapes fill each cell edge-to-edge. It is embedded in
the application binary so that NFO art renders identically on every platform,
regardless of the fonts installed on the host.
